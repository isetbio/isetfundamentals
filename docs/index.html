Our mission

To enable and enhance opportunities in the digital world for users with color vision deficiency.

Dalt-Net utilizes a deep-learning guided image transformation to modify visual content, like images and GUI, in both digital software and physical interfaces.

Simple & Effective

Dalt-Net's novel and guided Deep-Learning approach maintains simplicity and accuracy enhancing images, making it a state-of-the-art framework for both current apps and future research.

Practical & Implemented

Our product effectively fills the opportunity gap of Color Vision Deficient users through its efficient architecture and seamless integration into edge-end hardware.

Apps

Integration is essential to Dalt-Net. Here are our products:

Dalt-Vision for iPhone

Our multi-function app unifies the multi-functional capabilities of Dalt-Net into a product we call Dalt-Vision. This includes Augmented Reality software that can simulate and transform real-time camera input through Google Cardboard, and also a versatile user data-generation system for future research.
